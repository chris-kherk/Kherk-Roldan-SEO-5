What is Whitelabel SEO Partnership

Whitelabel SEO involves using your brand name and identity while outsourcing the actual SEO work to a partner company. As a reseller, you are not the one doing the SEO work; instead, you are outsourcing all the services to your Whitelabel SEO partnership, which is responsible for the actual work. This allows you to offer SEO services under your own brand name and identity.

Whitelabel SEO is similar to ghostwriting. When you work with a Whitelabel agency, you operate in the background, like a ghostwriter. Your reseller receives all the credit, as the client believes or knows that the reseller is working on their behalf. However, it is the Whitelabel SEO agency that is performing the work.

Cost-Effective Expertise

Leveraging the expertise of SEO professionals can be a cost-effective alternative to building an in-house SEO team. Building an SEO team from scratch can be challenging and time-consuming. It can take months or even years for team members to become true experts in SEO. Maintaining an in-house SEO team also requires significant overhead costs, such as rent, utilities, and employee benefits.

By outsourcing your SEO needs to experienced professionals, you can avoid these substantial investments and focus your resources on other critical aspects of your business. SEO experts have the knowledge and skills necessary to optimize your website and improve its visibility in search engine results without you needing to build and manage an internal team.

Client Acquisition and Management

As a Whitelabel provider, you don’t need to worry about getting clients – the reseller agency will handle that for you. Working with a Whitelabel agency is a win-win situation.

As a reseller, you’re responsible for finding the clients. Once you’ve secured the clients, the Whitelabel provider handles the actual work, such as delivering the services or products. Your role is to bring in clients and maintain the relationship, while the Whitelabel agency focuses on providing the quality services under your brand.

Example 1: Expanding Service Offerings

Suppose one of your clients requires extensive SEO services to enhance online visibility. However, your agency primarily specializes in Google Ads. In this scenario, you can collaborate with an SEO service provider to expand your service offering.

As an expert in Google Ads, you may not provide services outside of that domain. To address this, you can partner with a white-label SEO provider. This arrangement is a win-win for both parties. You don’t need to worry about training additional staff or hiring a large team, and the SEO provider can focus on delivering services like on-page optimization, link building, content creation, and technical SEO.

To get started, you’ll need to provide the SEO provider with details about your client. They will then conduct an SEO audit and create a strategy. The agency can then present this strategy to your client for approval, and the white-label SEO company can proceed with the implementation.

By partnering with an SEO specialist, you can expand your service offerings beyond just Google Ads, allowing you to better meet your clients’ needs.

Example 2: Outsourcing SEO Content

If you manage a digital marketing agency with clients who require multiple blog posts each month, you may choose to outsource SEO content to a Whitelabel partner. This allows you to regularly deliver search engine-optimized articles to all your clients.

However, if you cannot handle creating a large volume of SEO blog posts to meet your clients’ needs, you can outsource this work to an agency or SEO provider that offers optimized content services. This way, you can be recognized for producing content that performs well and increases organic traffic to your clients’ websites.

As a Whitelabel agency provider, your goal should be to create quality, SEO-optimized content that can drive organic results for your clients. You cannot simply create content without optimizing it – the content must be appealing, grammatically correct, and SEO-focused.

This arrangement also benefits your Whitelabel partner, as they can generate additional revenue without the need to handle client acquisition and management costs. Many Whitelabel SEO agencies focus on providing high-quality deliverables and results, but struggle with client acquisition. Partnering with you allows them to focus on the work while you handle the client-facing aspects.

The Process of Whitelabel SEO Partnership

A Whitelabel SEO provider, like the agency that wrote it, offers SEO services that can be resold by other agencies to their clients. The key points are:

  • The reseller agency does not have its own SEO services so that it can outsource the work to the Whitelabel provider.
  • The Whitelabel provider must be reliable and deliver good results.
  • The client pays $1,600 per month, and the reseller pays $600. So the reseller earns $1,000 without doing the work because they are just outsourcing.
  • The reseller can earn a profit by marking up the white label provider’s fees and selling the services to their clients.
  • This arrangement is a win-win for both the Whitelabel provider and the reseller.
